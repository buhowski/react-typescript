#### Dark Fantasy Odyssey / Action RPG Series

# Shadows of the Cossacks: Saga

A journey from the sacrificial pyres of 9th-century Kyivan Rus — through the blood-soaked era of the Cossacks — to an alternative future. Magical Middle Ages with otherworldly beasts and demonic baptisms give way to tribal cyber-futurism under the shadow of totalitarian tyranny. A large-scale transformation of evil that merely shifts its form alongside civilization.

At the epicenter lies a relentless struggle for the right to own one's destiny. Through enigmatic heroes resisting secretive sects, an author's reimagining of true history is unveiled: Church experiments, state control, and the Pope's Throne as the patron of reality.

The final choice leads to the destruction of religious institutions and triggers an extraordinary technological leap. No masters, no instructions, no ties to higher powers. For humanity, good and evil no longer exist — only the consequences of their choices.

Centuries later, upon the ruins of ancient gods, nations that never shared a common history will collide — and each will bring its own vision of freedom. Only time will determine who will be the next to raise the banner of eternal evil. The blood of cults pulses through the ages, carving out a single truth: war never ends.
