#### Dark Fantasy / Action RPG Series

# Shadows of the Cossacks: `The Untold Truth`

From pagan shrines and otherworldly beasts, through the demonic baptism of Kyivan Rus and the bloody era of the Cossacks — to the neon tyranny of tribal cyber-futurism.

A chronicle of resistance by those who refused to be fodder for the Church's experiments. The Popish Throne — patron of reality, clutching power with the demonic fist of dogma.

But deities shall fall. Idols will yield to self-belief — there shall be no good or evil, only the consequences of human decisions.

Nations with no shared history — Zaporozhian Cossacks, Vikings, Native Americans, African tribes — will converge on the ruins of an ancient pantheon, each with their own vision of freedom.

The blood of cults pulses through the ages, carving out a single truth: war never ends.
