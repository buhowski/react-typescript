#### Dark Fantasy Odyssey / Action RPG Series

# Shadows of the Cossacks: The Untold Truth

A saga. From pagan shrines and beasts of the beyond, through the demonic baptism of Kyivan Rus and the bloody era of the Cossacks — to the neon tyranny of tribal cyber-futurism.

A chronicle of resistance by those who refused to be fodder for the Church's experiments. The Papal Throne — patron of reality, clutching power with the demonic hand of dogma.

But the gods will fall. Idolatry will yield to self-belief — there will be no good or evil, only the consequences of human decisions. An evolutionary leap unforeseen by any cult.

The technological rupture will unite nations that never shared a common history — Zaporozhian Cossacks, Vikings, Native Americans, African tribes — each with their own vision of freedom.

The blood of cults pulses through the ages, carving out a single truth: war never ends.
