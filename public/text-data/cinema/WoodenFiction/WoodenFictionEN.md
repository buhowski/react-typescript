#### Animated Dark Crime Comedy

# Wooden Fiction

## Logline

Folk tale heroes in an uncompromising trepanation of Soviet myths: crime wars, aggressive absurdity, and a non-linear labyrinth of events where the fairy tale becomes a mirror of reality.

[mobile-slider]

## Concept

This is a satirical crime anthology that blends non-linear storytelling and sharp, Tarantino-style dialogue with gritty humor and the grotesque aesthetics of post-Soviet reality.

The plot unfolds in a grim yet recognizable world inhabited by reimagined characters from Ukrainian and Soviet fairy tales. Familiar figures are transformed into complex, morally ambiguous players entangled in criminal schemes.

The narrative consists of several intertwined stories, each evolving separately only to converge into a single plot. Timelines intersect, and the accidental decisions of one character trigger unpredictable consequences for others.

Behind the chaotic plot and dark humor lies a biting satire — how corruption decays even the purest archetypes, and how ideals, distorted over time, become weapons.
